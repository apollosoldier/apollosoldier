WifiBot

The Wifibot M platforms are characterized above all by a great flexibility allowing the robots to be used in multiple environments and situations. The articulated mechanical design and their six wheel drive allow the Wifibot M to evolve outdoor over rough terrain and even overcome obstacles up to15cm high. The relatively small dimension and weight make it easily transportable while remaining suitable for all kind of uses and applications as they offer many expansion possibilities at different levels. Wifibot M Lab version include a very capable industrial board under Linux or Windows XP featuring a large choice of accessible interfaces like Ethernet, RS232, USB2, keyboard and mouse input, VGA output, and wireless networking capabilities (wi-fi), ideal for multi-robot applications. An IP camera, a battery charger and a robot control interface are included. Additional optional components such a GPS, IR range sensors or an ethernet-serial bridge can be integrated upon request.
